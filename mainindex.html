Quality Management Unit

About

The Quality Management Unit in UiTM Kedah is responsible for ensuring consistency and excellence in the quality of education, services, and processes within the university. The unit collaborates with various departments and units to develop and implement quality management strategies and initiatives. Here are three key functions of the Quality Management Unit in UiTM Kedah:

Consistency in Consultation: The Quality Unit works closely with departments and units across UiTM Kedah to establish consistent practices and standards. They provide consultation and guidance to ensure that quality is maintained and improved in various areas such as teaching and learning, research, student services, and administrative processes. By fostering collaboration and sharing best practices, the QMU promotes a culture of continuous improvement throughout the university.

Quality Competition Awards: The QMU organizes and manages quality competitions at the university level. These competitions aim to recognize and reward outstanding achievements in quality management. By acknowledging and celebrating successful practices, the QMU encourages departments and units to strive for excellence and contribute to the overall quality enhancement of UiTM Kedah. The awards serve as a motivation for continuous improvement and innovation.

Management of Quality Records: The QMU plays a crucial role in managing quality records within UiTM Kedah. They establish systems and processes to ensure the proper documentation, storage, and retrieval of quality-related information, including policies, guidelines, procedures, and audit reports. Effective management of quality records enables transparency, accountability, and the ability to track progress in quality initiatives. The QMU may also facilitate the implementation of quality management systems and standards, such as ISO 9001, to ensure compliance and alignment with international best practices.

Overall, the Quality Management Unit in UiTM Kedah is dedicated to promoting a culture of quality and continuous improvement. By fostering collaboration, recognizing excellence, and ensuring proper documentation, the QMU contributes to the enhancement of education, services, and processes at UiTM Kedah.
